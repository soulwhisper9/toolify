Frequently asked questions

How do I calculate percentage of a number?
Multiply the number by the percentage and divide by 100. For example, 20% of 500 = (20 × 500) ÷ 100 = 100.
How do I calculate percentage change?
Subtract old value from new value, divide by old value, multiply by 100. Example: from 200 to 250 = (250-200)/200 × 100 = 25% increase.
