Frequently asked questions

How does the word counter work?
It splits your text by spaces and line breaks in real-time using JavaScript. No server involved — everything happens instantly in your browser.
What is reading time based on?
Average adult reading speed of 238 words per minute (WPM). This is the standard used by Medium and most content platforms.
Does this work for Urdu or Arabic text?
Yes — it counts any Unicode text including Urdu, Arabic, and other languages by splitting on whitespace.
Is there a character limit?
No limit at all. Paste an entire book if you want — it handles large text files without slowing down.
