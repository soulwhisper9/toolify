Frequently asked questions

What is Nisab?
Nisab is the minimum amount of wealth a Muslim must have before Zakat becomes obligatory. It equals 87.48g of gold or 612.36g of silver, whichever is lower.
Is Zakat on gold calculated on jewellery worn daily?
According to most scholars, yes — Zakat applies to all gold above Nisab including jewellery. Some scholars exempt regularly worn jewellery. Consult your local scholar.
What is the Zakat rate?
Zakat is 2.5% of your total Zakatable wealth held for one full lunar year (Hawl).
Is house or car included in Zakat?
No. Your primary home, car and personal items for daily use are not included. Only wealth held for investment or trade is Zakatable.
