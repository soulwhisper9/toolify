Frequently asked questions

Is this image compressor really free?
Yes, completely free. No signup, no limits, no watermarks — ever.
Are my images uploaded to a server?
No. All compression happens inside your browser using JavaScript. Your images never leave your device.
What quality setting should I use?
75% is ideal for most uses — it reduces file size by 50–70% with no visible quality loss. Use 60% for web thumbnails and 85%+ for print.
Which formats are supported?
JPG, PNG, and WebP. Output is always in the same format as the input.
