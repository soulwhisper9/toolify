Frequently asked questions

What is the tax-free limit for salaried persons in Pakistan 2025-26?
For FY 2025-26, income up to PKR 600,000 per year is exempt from income tax for salaried individuals.
How is income tax calculated in Pakistan?
Pakistan uses a progressive slab system. Different rates apply to different portions of your income. This calculator applies each slab rate to the relevant portion automatically.
Is this calculator official?
This is an indicative tool based on FBR 2025-26 Finance Act slabs. For official tax assessment, file your return on Iris (iris.fbr.gov.pk) or consult a tax advisor.
