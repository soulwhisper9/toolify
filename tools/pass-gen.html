Frequently asked questions

Are generated passwords stored anywhere?
No. Passwords are generated using your browser's cryptographic random number generator. Nothing is sent to any server.
What length password should I use?
16 characters is strong for most accounts. Use 24+ for banking, email, and password managers.
What does "exclude ambiguous" mean?
It removes characters like 0, O, l, 1, I that look similar and cause confusion when reading passwords.
