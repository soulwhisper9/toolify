Frequently asked questions

What is EMI?
EMI (Equated Monthly Installment) is a fixed payment you make to a bank every month to repay a loan. It includes both principal and interest portions.
What is the current home loan rate in Pakistan?
As of 2026, home loan rates in Pakistan range from 20% to 23% per annum depending on the bank and whether it is conventional or Islamic financing.
How can I reduce my EMI?
You can reduce EMI by increasing loan tenure, making a larger down payment, or negotiating a lower interest rate. Use the sliders above to compare scenarios.
