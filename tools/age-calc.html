Frequently asked questions

How is exact age calculated?
We calculate the difference between your date of birth and the target date in years, months, and days accounting for leap years and varying month lengths.
What if I want to know my age on a specific past or future date?
Use the "Age at date" field. Enter any past or future date and we'll calculate exactly how old you were or will be on that day.
How is zodiac sign determined?
Western/tropical zodiac signs are assigned based on the month and day of your birth date.
